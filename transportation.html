Directions to National Central University

The 2nd International Conference on Metaverse and AI Companions in Education and Society

Directions to National Central University

By Car:

National Highway No. 1 (Sun Yat-sen Freeway):
Exit at Zhongli Interchange (62 km mark), head towards Xinwu, follow Minzu Road, turn right onto Sanmin Road, turn left onto Zhongzheng Road, then turn left onto Zhongda Road to reach the main entrance of the university. The drive takes about 5-10 minutes.

National Highway No. 3 (Formosa Freeway):
Exit at Daxi Interchange (62 km mark), head towards Zhongli, transfer to Provincial Expressway No. 66 (towards Zhongli and Guanyin), merge onto National Highway No. 1 (northbound), exit at Zhongli Interchange (62 km mark), head towards Xinwu, follow Minzu Road, turn right onto Sanmin Road, turn left onto Zhongzheng Road, then turn left onto Zhongda Road to reach the main entrance of the university. The drive takes about 20 minutes.

GPS Coordinates:

Latitude: 24.96828; Longitude: 121.195474

By High-Speed Rail (HSR):

Get off at Taoyuan HSR Station, take Bus No. 172 or 173 from Platform 8 of the bus station directly to National Central University (buses run approximately every hour, 15-20 minute drive), or take a bus heading towards Zhongli (transfer to a city bus heading to the university at the city bus terminal), or take a taxi or drive to the university.

City Bus:

City buses No. 132, 133, and 172 run between Zhongli City and National Central University, taking about 20-30 minutes. Some routes also pass through Taoyuan HSR Station. Campus bus stops include: Main Gate Security Room, Zhi-Xi Building, Yi-Ren Hall, Rear Entrance, and Observatory of Central University. Payment Methods: Cash or electronic tickets (EasyCard or Taiwan Easy Go). Swipe the card when boarding and alighting. One-section fare: NT$18 for a full fare, NT$9 for a half fare.

Physical Venue:

Room A102, 1st Floor, Engineering Building 5, National Central University (NCU)
